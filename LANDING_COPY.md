# FAQ

## Does AuditEX connect directly to billing systems?

Not in the MVP. The current version uses manual input so teams can run an audit quickly without giving access to finance systems or company cards.

---

## Will the audit recommend cancelling every AI tool?

No. The goal is not to remove tools blindly. If the stack already looks efficient, the audit says so instead of forcing unnecessary recommendations.

---

## Which tools are currently supported?

The audit currently supports major AI products including ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, OpenAI API, Anthropic API, Windsurf, NVIDIA NIM, and v0.

---

## How are savings calculated?

Savings calculations use fixed audit rules and pricing logic. AI is only used to generate readable summaries and recommendations from the audit results.

---

## What happens if the audit finds significant waste?

If the report identifies meaningful savings opportunities, the platform recommends a deeper vendor and workflow review through Credex.

---

## Who is this built for?

AuditEX is designed for startups and growing teams that adopted AI tools quickly and now want visibility into where money is being wasted or duplicated.
