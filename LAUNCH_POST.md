## Short Blog Post

Most startups now have AI spend spread across ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, Copilot, Gemini, API vendors, and builder tools. The problem is not that teams use too much AI; it is that nobody owns the benchmark. A founder sees a monthly bill, assumes the tool is necessary, and moves on.

AuditEX is a free AI spend audit for founders, finance leads, and engineering managers. Enter your AI tools, plan, seats, team size, use case, and monthly spend. In a few seconds, AuditEX shows where spend is efficient, where seats are duplicated, where a plan may be oversized, and where API workloads or credits deserve review.

The report is built to be board-ready: total monthly savings, annual savings, per-tool reasoning, a personalized AI summary, PDF export, and a public share link with clean previews. If the audit shows material savings, the result points teams toward a Credex consultation so the estimate can become vendor action.

Try it before your next renewal cycle. The best outcome might be savings. The second-best outcome is knowing your AI stack is already disciplined.
