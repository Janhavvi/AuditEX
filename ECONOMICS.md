# Economics

Assumptions are rough because Credex pricing and conversion data are not public in this repo. The model below treats AuditEX as a lead-generation and qualification funnel for Credex, not as a standalone paid SaaS product.

Converted lead value: assume Credex earns revenue when a qualified company purchases credits, financing, or a related vendor/workflow review product. If a high-savings audit shows $500-$2,000/month in avoidable spend, the company is showing both budget pain and willingness to act. A conservative converted lead value is $2,500 gross profit: for example, a $10,000 financed/credit-related transaction or advisory package at 25 percent gross margin. A stronger case might be $7,500-$15,000 if the relationship expands into larger credit purchases.

Channel CAC estimates from the GTM plan:

| Channel | Cash CAC | Time cost | Expected completed audits | Notes |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- |
| Founder/operator DMs | $0 | 10 hours per 100 DMs | 10-20 | Best early channel because outreach can be specific |
| Community teardown posts | $0 | 3 hours/post | 5-15 | Works if the post gives real numbers, not generic tips |
| Fractional CFO partners | $0 | 5 hours to recruit first 3 | 10-30 | Higher trust, slower setup |
| Public report referrals | $0 | product-led | 5-20 | Depends on reports feeling share-worthy |

If founder/operator time is valued at $50/hour, effective CAC might be $25-$100 per completed audit early. If 20 percent of completed audits show high savings and 25 percent of high-savings leads book a consultation, then 100 completed audits produce 5 consultations. If 20 percent of consultations convert to credit purchase, that is 1 converted customer. At $2,500 gross profit per customer, the funnel supports up to $25 CAC per completed audit. At $7,500 gross profit, it supports up to $75 CAC per completed audit.

Profitability threshold:

- Audit completed to Credex consultation: 5 percent minimum at $2,500 converted value
- Consultation to credit purchase: 20 percent minimum
- Combined audit to purchase: 1 percent
- At 1 percent conversion and $2,500 gross profit, each completed audit is worth $25 gross profit

For the tool to drive $1M ARR in 18 months, one of two things must be true. Either Credex monetizes converted customers at about $10,000 ARR and AuditEX produces 100 converted customers, or it monetizes at $25,000 ARR and needs 40 converted customers. At a 1 percent audit-to-purchase conversion rate, that means 4,000-10,000 completed audits over 18 months. At a stronger 3 percent conversion rate, the target drops to roughly 1,350-3,350 completed audits. The most important lever is not traffic volume; it is increasing the share of audits that are both high-savings and ready for a financial product conversation.
